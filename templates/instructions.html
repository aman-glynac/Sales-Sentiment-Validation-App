üîç What to Look For

As you review each deal, pay attention to these key elements:

  • Deal Progression: Is the deal moving forward, stagnating, or regressing? Look for concrete signs of advancement.
  • Client Engagement: How engaged is the client? Are they responding positively, asking questions, showing interest?

  • Opportunity Indicators: Identify positive signals like client enthusiasm, referrals, or expansion discussions.

  • 2 - Poor: The AI's analysis has significant errors or misses important points
